Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 21, 2026

App Usage Per Day is designed to help users track daily app screen time for selected apps on their own device. We respect your privacy and keep the app simple and local.

Data Collection

App Usage Per Day does not collect, upload, sell, or share personal data. Selected apps, groups, notes, and chart settings are stored locally on your device.

Usage Access Permission

The app may request Android Usage Access permission. This permission is used only to calculate daily foreground usage time for apps that you choose to track. The app does not read the content inside other apps, messages, passwords, notifications, browsing content, or private files.

Network and Third Parties

The app does not require an account and does not include ads, analytics SDKs, or third-party tracking SDKs.

User Control

You can deny or revoke Usage Access at any time in Android Settings. If the permission is revoked, the app stops calculating charts.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer through the contact information shown on Google Play.
